Basic Lasagna with Meat Sauce Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1 lb ground beef
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (28 oz) crushed tomatoes
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 1 egg
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese

Instructions

  1. Cook ground beef, onion, and garlic until browned.
  2. Add crushed tomatoes and simmer for 15-20 minutes.
  3. Cook lasagna noodles according to package instructions.
  4. Mix ricotta cheese, egg, and Parmesan cheese.
  5. Assemble lasagna: meat sauce, noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, mozzarella cheese. Repeat.
  6. Cover with foil and bake at 375°F (190°C) for 25 minutes.
  7. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes.
