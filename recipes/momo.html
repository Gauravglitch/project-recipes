Mo:mo Recipe

Servings: 20-25 momos

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon baking powder
  • 1/4 cup ghee or oil
  • 1/2 cup lukewarm water
  • Filling ingredients (see below)

Filling:

  • 1 pound ground meat (beef, pork, or chicken)
  • 1 onion, finely chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 tablespoon ginger paste
  • 1 tablespoon soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon sesame oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped cilantro or scallions for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Make the dough by combining flour, salt, and baking powder in a bowl. Add ghee or oil and mix until the dough comes together. Gradually add lukewarm water and knead for 5-7 minutes until smooth.
  2. Prepare the filling by mixing all the filling ingredients together in a bowl.
  3. Assemble the momos by dividing the dough into small balls. Roll out each ball into a thin circle. Place a tablespoon of filling in the center of each circle. Fold the dough over the filling to form a half-moon shape and press the edges together to seal.
  4. Steam the momos by placing them in a steamer basket lined with parchment paper. Steam for 15-20 minutes or until the dough is cooked and the filling is hot.
  5. Serve hot with your favorite dipping sauce. Garnish with chopped cilantro or scallions.

Note: You can also pan-fry the momos before steaming for a crispy bottom. Simply heat a tablespoon of oil in a pan over medium heat and cook the momos for 2-3 minutes on each side before steaming.
