Noodle Recipe

Servings: 4-6 people

Ingredients:

  • 1 package of noodles (your choice of type and brand)
  • 2 tablespoons of vegetable oil
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 1 cup of mixed vegetables (e.g. bell peppers, carrots, broccoli)
  • 2 cups of chicken or beef broth
  • 1 teaspoon of soy sauce (optional)
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Step 1: Cook the noodles according to the package instructions. Drain and set aside.
  2. Step 2: Heat the oil in a large skillet or wok over medium-high heat.
  3. Step 3: Add the onion and garlic and cook until the onion is translucent.
  4. Step 4: Add the mixed vegetables and cook until they are tender-crisp.
  5. Step 5: Add the broth and soy sauce (if using) and bring to a boil.
  6. Step 6: Add the cooked noodles to the skillet or wok and stir to combine.
  7. Step 7: Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Step 8: Serve hot and enjoy!

Note: You can customize this recipe to your taste by adding your favorite protein (e.g. chicken, beef, tofu) or using different vegetables.
